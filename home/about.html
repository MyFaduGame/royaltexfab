Welcome To Royal TexFab

Royal TexFab, established in 2006, has its own history.

We started manufacturing polyester fabric prints and dyed in 2006. Today Royal TexFab have several varieties and products.

Under Royal TexFab, manufacturing of material was started. Today Royal TexFab has a wide variety of products in its portfolio – Single Jersey, Interlock, Modal, Loop Knit to Cotton Spandex, the later being Royal TexFab’s premium product.

We offer our products in a wide variety of designs that are in sync with prevailing trends. We strive hard to constantly upgrade ourselves so as to stay ahead of market. Owing to our emphasis on ethical business dealings, we have successfully garnered the trust of our esteemed customers.
